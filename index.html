About AI Ethics Education

AI Ethics Education is a platform for learning about the ethical considerations surrounding artificial intelligence. As AI becomes increasingly integrated into our daily lives, it is important to consider the potential ethical implications of these technologies. AI Ethics Education provides a comprehensive introduction to these issues, covering topics such as bias, privacy, and accountability.

What We Offer

Introduction to Machine Learning

Learn about the basics of machine learning, including supervised and unsupervised learning, classification, and regression.

Programming with AI

Learn how to program with AI, including using popular libraries such as TensorFlow and PyTorch.

Ethical Considerations in Smart Cities

Explore the ethical considerations surrounding the use of AI in smart cities, including issues of privacy and surveillance.
